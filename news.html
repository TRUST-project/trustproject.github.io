News & Events

December 15, 2024

TRUST Team Presents at International Bioethics Conference

Our team recently presented preliminary findings from the longitudinal study at the International Bioethics Conference held in Madrid. Dr. Martínez-López delivered a keynote address on trust dynamics in healthcare networks, while Jorge Suárez presented methodological innovations in network analysis.

November 8, 2024

Workshop on Social Network Analysis in Granada

We organized a two-day workshop on advanced social network analysis techniques for healthcare research. The event brought together researchers from across Spain and featured hands-on sessions on data collection and network visualization tools.

October 20, 2024

Collaboration Meeting with University of Yamaguchi

Dr. Janet Delgado facilitated a virtual collaboration meeting between our team and researchers at the University of Yamaguchi. We discussed cross-cultural perspectives on trust in healthcare systems and planned future joint research initiatives.

September 12, 2024

Presentation at EASP General Meeting in Barcelona

The TRUST team presented preliminary findings on network interventions at the European Association of Social Psychology meeting. Our presentation on "Trust Networks in Organ Donation Decisions" received positive feedback and sparked interesting discussions with international colleagues.

August 5, 2024

New Research Grant Awarded

We're excited to announce that the TRUST Project has been awarded a new research grant from the Spanish Ministry of Science. This funding will support the expansion of our longitudinal study and enable us to include additional participant groups.

July 18, 2024

Launch of "Social Networks & Health" Study

We officially launched our large-scale longitudinal study examining social networks and health decision-making. We are currently recruiting participants for the first wave of data collection. Interested individuals can find more information on our Active Studies page.
